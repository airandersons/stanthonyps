About Our School

Home of quality education since 1995

St. Anthony’s Nursery & Primary School – Mutungo

Founded with a vision to provide exceptional education to the children of Mutungo and beyond, our school has grown into a beloved community institution. We blend academic excellence with strong moral values, creating a nurturing environment where every child is known, valued, and empowered.

Located on Biina Road, our campus offers a safe, stimulating setting with modern classrooms, outdoor play areas, and specialist facilities for music and creative arts. We serve children from nursery through primary seven, preparing them not just for exams, but for life.

GOD WITH US is not just our badge – it’s the foundation of our daily life.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of holistic education, shaping confident, compassionate leaders who transform their communities.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, inclusive, and excellent education through innovative teaching, moral guidance, and a supportive environment.
