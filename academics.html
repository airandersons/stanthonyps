Academics

Building strong foundations for lifelong learning

Early Years (Nursery)

Our nursery program provides a warm, stimulating environment where children aged 3–5 develop social skills, curiosity, and a love for learning. Through play-based activities, storytelling, and guided exploration, we nurture each child's cognitive, emotional, and physical growth.

  • Child‑centered, play‑based curriculum
  • Creative arts and sensory play
  • Healthy snack time & outdoor play
  • Low teacher‑to‑child ratio

Primary School

Lower Primary (P1–P3)

Building literacy, numeracy, and foundational skills. Learners enjoy interactive lessons, group activities, and continuous assessment in a caring environment.

Upper Primary (P4–P7)

Deeper subject mastery, critical thinking, and preparation for national exams. Emphasis on leadership, science projects, and personal responsibility.

Across all primary grades, we follow the national curriculum enriched with our own programs in music, computing, and values education. Small class sizes ensure that every child receives individual support.
