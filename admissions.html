Begin your child's journey with St. Anthony's

Join our school family

St. Anthony's Nursery & Primary School welcomes children from age 3 through Primary Seven. We believe every child is unique and deserves an education that nurtures their talents and character. Our admissions process is simple, transparent, and designed to help you and your child feel at home.

We offer both day and boarding options for upper primary, with flexible fee structures and a range of payment plans. Places are limited, so we encourage early application.
